Our Beginnings

Our BeginningsNamaste Café began as a dream shared by Priya and Raj Sharma, who moved from Kathmandu to start a new life while bringing a piece of their homeland with them. After years of working in the hospitality industry, they decided to create a space that would showcase the rich cultural heritage of Nepal through its cuisine and ambiance.

In 2018, they transformed a small corner shop into what is now known as Namaste Café - a haven for tea enthusiasts, coffee lovers, and anyone seeking a moment of tranquility in their busy day.

Real Food. No Secret Ingredients.

A table full of warmth, tradition, and stories.

At Namaste Café, we honor Nepal’s rich tea culture through a colorful spread of spiced chiyas, herbal infusions, and handmade sweets.
Each cup is steeped in heritage and served with care — whether it’s a bold masala chai or a delicate herbal blend.

Surrounded by traditional utensils, seasonal treats, and a spirit of togetherness,
this table invites you to slow down, savor the moment, and feel right at home.
It’s more than just tea — it’s a taste of where we come from.
